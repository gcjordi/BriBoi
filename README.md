# BriBoi

## Biodiversity Risk Intelligence (BRI) + Biodiversity Opportunity Intelligence (BOI)

BriBoi is an open-source initiative created by Jordi Garcia Castillón / CibraLAB.

### Mission
Use Artificial Intelligence, open data and scientific evidence to:
- Detect biodiversity and environmental risks (BRI)
- Discover biodiversity improvement opportunities (BOI)
- Support citizens, researchers, NGOs and public administrations

## Components

### BRI
Risk monitoring platform:
- Climate stress
- Biodiversity degradation
- Invasive species
- Environmental health signals
- Early warning indicators

### BOI
Opportunity discovery platform:
- Habitat restoration
- Pollinator initiatives
- Ecological corridors
- Nature-based solutions
- Biodiversity enhancement projects

## Principles
- Open Science
- Explainable AI
